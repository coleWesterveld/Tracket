Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

Overview

Tracket is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. Everything you log in Tracket — your workouts, programs, sets, reps, and notes — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account credentials.
  • We do not collect health or fitness data from Apple Health or any other source.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or any third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not use cookies or any web tracking technologies.
  • We do not access your location, contacts, camera, or microphone.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Tracket is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device using Flutter's local storage. This data is subject to your device's standard backup policies (e.g. iCloud backup) if you have those enabled — this is outside our control and governed by Apple's privacy practices.

When you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

Program Sharing

If you choose to share a program with another user, the sharing mechanism is performed directly between devices (e.g. via a share sheet). We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process this data.

Third-Party Services

Tracket does not integrate with or transmit data to any third-party services, advertisers, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

Tracket does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no information from anyone, this applies equally to all users.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a material way (e.g. if a future version adds any data collection), we will update this page and revise the effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
